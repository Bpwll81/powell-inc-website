Smart Carts: Waste on the Go

Waste management doesn't just happen at a stationary bin—it happens during transit, table busing, and banquet breakdown. Our mobile Smart Carts are equipped with the same gravimetric technology as our stationary shelves.

As staff move through banquet halls and dining floors, the AI tracks discarded weight in real-time, assigning loss metrics dynamically. This turns a previously unmeasurable variable into highly actionable cost-control data.

V-Scanners & V-Printers

Bridging the physical-to-digital divide requires seamless front-of-house execution. Our approved handheld V-Scanners utilize fast QR and Barcode scanning to track physical items as they move from the loading dock, through prep stations, and onto the dining floor.

This real-time chain of custody tracks prep stages and waste dynamically while automatically logging HACCP compliance—eliminating manual clipboards. Once a cycle completes, V-Printers generate cryptographic receipts that perfectly mirror the immutable ledger.

V-Lens & Thermal Arrays

The Varis ecosystem sees and feels the environment. The V-Lens provides an overhead visual AI heuristic to track anomalous inventory movement, actively deterring internal shrinkage and theft.

Paired with integrated Thermal Gauges inside walk-ins and freezers, the ecosystem monitors ambient temperatures 24/7. If a compressor fails or a door is left ajar, Varis OS instantly flags the anomaly—ensuring absolute, zero-lapse HACCP compliance and preventing catastrophic inventory loss.
