Our Philosophy

People Over Profit

We believe the hospitality industry lost its way when it started treating people like line items. Powell Inc. is a family-first holding company dedicated to restoring the dignity of service.

True hospitality starts with the employee. When we prioritize the well-being of our teams, we create an environment where success is shared, not extracted.
