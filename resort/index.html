Hotel Ilona: Family Sanctuary

Designed as a physical bridge between elite hospitality and autonomous finance, Hotel Ilona integrates private social hubs with high-fidelity biometric security.

  • Integrated Knee Hockey Arena: Located within the hotel, the arena serves as a private social core for players and families to bond.
  • Lifetime V-Band Assets: Every guest receives a V-Band meant to be kept—a permanent badge of belonging. Keep it year-round for biometric measurements, notifications, and real-time sync with resort activities.
  • Child Independence: Parents can load funds directly onto their children's V-Bands, granting them the freedom to purchase fresh, healthy lunches at the Mercantile independently.
  • Biometric Unlock: Proximity sensors activate a biometric PIN or optical fingerprint scanner for suite entry. For absolute privacy, proximity sensing is automatically disabled the moment you depart Resort grounds.

The Outermost Ring: Foundation Center

Knowledge is the first circle of defense. The Foundation Center is the outermost ring of the complex because education comes before anything.

Enrollment begins at age 8. We believe early childhood is sacred, allowing parents to mold a child's moral compass and values at home. Once that character is set, the Center provides training in:

  • The Core: Intensive study in reading, writing, arithmetic, and the sciences.
  • Self-Sustenance: Passion-based learning focused on homesteading and hunting.
  • Self-Governance: Ensuring future generations possess the specific knowledge required to self-govern and maintain institutional freedom.

The Inner Ring: Sibling Play Hub

A multi-rink facility naturally becomes rowdy; the inner ring serves as a high-energy world for children to explore, giving active siblings a dedicated sanctuary.

  • Integrated Hub: Professional-grade indoor climbing, active play arrays, and gated transitions to protected outdoor playgrounds.
  • Viewing Hubs: Parents sit in designated hubs within this ring to monitor the ice while keeping children engaged in safe recreation.
  • Family Sync: Monitor child wellness and location at the rink via real-time V-Band updates delivered directly to your device.
