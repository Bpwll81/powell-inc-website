Hotel Ilona: Family Sovereignty

This is where the mission statement on our main page comes to full fruition. A physical sanctuary decoupled from legacy banking, powered by autonomous logic and asset-backed settlement.

  • A Lifetime Key: All guests receive a V-Band. This is meant to be kept—a permanent badge of belonging. Use it year-round for biometric measurements, wellness tracking, and updates about upcoming reservations or events.
  • Child Independence: Parents can load money directly onto their children's V-Bands, allowing them to purchase fresh, healthy lunches at the Mercantile. Monitor their status while they are at the rink for practice or enjoying a game.
  • The Player's Social Core: The hotel architecture explicitly houses the Integrated Knee Hockey Arena and private lounges, providing a secure athletic hub for team bonding and social sanctuary.
  • Biometric Security & Privacy: Proximity sensors activate a PIN or fingerprint scanner for suite entry. For absolute privacy, all proximity sensing is automatically disabled the moment you depart from Resort grounds.
