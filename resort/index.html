The Ice Campus (Recessed Topography)

Sitting on a recessed, lower topographic level below the Main Hub are 5 individual, dedicated ice arenas. Guests transition down to the ice level via a series of clear vertical shafts featuring leisurely walkways, escalators, and ADA-compliant elevators. Once at the ice level, guests can navigate between the 5 rinks via landscaped trails or an underground shuttle system.
