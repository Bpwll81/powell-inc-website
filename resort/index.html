Designed as a physical bridge between elite hospitality and autonomous finance, Hotel Ilona is a sanctuary for restoring family sovereignty.

Architectural mockups in design. The V-Plex is engineered as a layered ecosystem of massive concentric rings surrounding a central ice campus building.

The Outermost Ring: Foundation Center

Knowledge is the bedrock of sovereignty. The outermost protective ring must ensure education comes before anything. Enrollment begins at age 8.

  • Core Foundations: Rigorous study in reading, writing, arithmetic, and the sciences.
  • Self-Sustenance: Passion-based learning focused on homesteading, gardening, and hunting.
  • Governance: Specific knowledge required to master self-government and physical freedom.

Core Pillars: Knowledge, Self-Governance, Athletics, Self-Reliance

The Inner Ring: Sibling Play Hub

A multi-rink environment can naturally become rowdy; the inner ring provides active siblings a world of their own to explore, giving them safe sanctuary without overcrowding the commons.

  • Integrated Hub: Wall climbing, active play arrays, and gated outdoor playgrounds.
  • Viewing Hubs: Sit within this ring to monitor the ice building and remain synchronized with children via real-time V-Band updates.
