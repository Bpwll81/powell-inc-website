LEDGER INTEGRATION

Proximity Nodes & Micro-Buys

The V-Band isn't just an RFID room key—it is a secure physical node bridging the guest to the Unity Protocol. Proprietary proximity sensors detect your approach, activating biometric PIN or fingerprint scanners for seamless room entry. Every tap-to-pay transaction at the Mercantile, and every secure door entry, is processed by Varis OS in real-time.

These physical interactions generate constant operational data. When a guest authenticates to unlock their suite or authorize a spa session, the system registers a state-change that triggers automated micro-buys and burns on the distributed ledger. This ensures continuous, autonomous network volume driven purely by physical resort movement.
