The Resort

A Closed-Circuit Financial Sanctuary

  • The Lifelong V-Band: This is your permanent key to the ecosystem. It is meant to be kept and used whenever you return. Beyond the Resort, it remains a vital tool for biometric measurements and real-time notifications for upcoming events.
  • Community & Family Sovereignty: Peace of mind is engineered into the campus. Monitor your children during their hours of practice or while they enjoy a game at the Ice Campus—even during the school day—with secure, encrypted updates sent directly to your V-Band.
