Hotel Ilona: Family Sanctuary

Designed as the pinnacle of hospitality, Hotel Ilona integrates private social hubs with high-fidelity biometric security and omnipresent energy grids.

  • Integrated Knee Hockey Arena: Located directly within the hotel architecture, the arena serves as a private social core for players and families to interact.
  • Lifetime V-Band Assets: Every guest receives a V-Band meant to be kept. It provides year-round biometric measurements, notifications for upcoming events, and real-time synchronization with hotel and rink activities.
  • Child Safety & Monitoring: Parents can load money directly onto their children's V-Bands for Mercantile use. Use your band to monitor siblings at the rink or in the sibling zone during practice or games.
  • Biometric Unlock: Proximity sensors activate a biometric PIN or fingerprint scanner for suite entry. For absolute privacy, proximity sensing is automatically disabled the moment you depart from Resort grounds.

The Outermost Ring: Foundation Center

Knowledge is the first circle of defense. The Foundation Center is the outermost ring of the complex because education comes before anything.

Enrollment begins at age 8. We believe early childhood is sacred, allowing parents to mold a child's moral compass and values at home. Once that character is set, the Center provides rigorous training in:

  • The Core: Intensive study in reading, writing, arithmetic, and the sciences.
  • Self-Sustenance: Vocational training in homesteading, hunting, and self-sustaining systems.
  • Sovereign Governance: Passion-based learning designed to ensure future generations have the tools to self-govern.
