Hotel Ilona: Family Sanctuary

Designed as the pinnacle of hospitality, Hotel Ilona integrates private social hubs with high-fidelity biometric security and omnipresent energy grids[cite: 227, 278].

  • Integrated Knee Hockey Arena: Located directly within the hotel architecture, the arena serves as a private social core for players and families to interact.
  • Lifetime V-Band Assets: Every guest receives a V-Band meant to be kept[cite: 227]. It provides year-round biometric measurements[cite: 276], notifications for upcoming events, and real-time synchronization with hotel and rink activities.
  • OTA Charging Grid: Fed by the central Tesla Tower, an Over-The-Air (OTA) power delivery network blankets the resort[cite: 278]. Using rectenna technology (comprising a matching network and Schottky diode)[cite: 279, 356], your V-Band and personal Qi-capable devices stay continuously powered ambiently.
  • Child Safety & Monitoring: Use your V-Band to monitor siblings at the rink or in the sibling zone during practice or games[cite: 227]. All proximity sensing is automatically disabled the moment you depart from Resort grounds to ensure absolute privacy[cite: 277].
  • Biometric Unlock: Proximity sensors within a 3.0-meter radius [cite: 277] activate biometric PIN or optical fingerprint scanners [cite: 276] for secure, cable-free room entry.

The Vaitukaitis Complex (V-Plex)

The V-Plex is composed of two massive concentric rings that surround the primary Ice Campus building, creating a layered ecosystem of education, safety, and elite athletics.

The Outermost Ring: Foundation Center

Knowledge is the bedrock of sovereignty. The Foundation Center serves as the primary ring because education comes before athletics.

Enrollment is focused on ages 10 and older. We believe early childhood is sacred, allowing parents to mold a child's moral compass and values at home. Once that character is set, the Center provides rigorous training in:

  • Self-Sustainability: Intensive courses in homesteading and hunting.
  • Sovereign Governance: Deep study of arithmetic, reading, writing, sciences, and constitutional law.
  • Passion-Based Learning: Specialized focus areas to empower the next generation to self-govern.
