III. Tactical Due Diligence Rebuttals

"What if an exchange refuses a Day-1 listing?"

Rebuttal (Conceded): We concede that a Day-1 listing refusal is not make-or-break. We understand security concerns are paramount for exchanges and are happy to work directly with the investor to find a mutually beneficial solution that ensures UNT circulation begins immediately upon audit clearance.

"Is this just another algorithmic stablecoin risk?"

Rebuttal: Impossible. UGLD is only minted at 5,000 oz intervals and is backed 1:1 by physical gold held in Bail-Free vaults. It is not supported or backed by fiat or speculation; 1 UGLD will always equal 1 oz of physical gold.

"How is founder exit/dump risk mitigated?"

Rebuttal: Founders are cryptographically barred from principal liquidation. Only bi-monthly staking rewards are permitted via the Smart Waterfall Protocol, ensuring zero market dump potential.

"What protection exists if the parent entity fails?"

Rebuttal: Assets are held in ring-fenced SPVs. Token holders are legally defined as Senior Secured Creditors with explicit redemption rights to the underlying physical gold.

"Is UNT classified as an unregistered security?"

Rebuttal: No. UNT is strictly a utility token designed for network governance and hardware fees. Its alignment with the Colorado Digital Token Act and EU MiCA standards is a core priority.

"Is the codebase secure and audited?"

Rebuttal: Deployment is locked until a Tier-1 audit firm certifies zero critical vulnerabilities. We have allocated specific funds for the investor to manage this audit directly.
