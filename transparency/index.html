III. Institutional Due Diligence Rebuttals

"Is UNT classified as an unregistered security?"

Rebuttal: No. UNT is strictly a utility token for network governance and gas fees. It carries no equity rights or dividends, ensuring alignment with the Colorado Digital Token Act and EU MiCA standards.

"What if Tier-1 exchanges delay or refuse listing?"

Rebuttal: CEX listings are tertiary. If delayed, we deploy via our proprietary **Unity Institutional Node**, allowing partners to facilitate peer-to-peer settlement within our ring-fenced LGE environment.

"How is founder 'exit-scam' or dump risk mitigated?"

Rebuttal: Founders are cryptographically barred from principal liquidation. Only bi-monthly staking rewards are permitted for execution via the Smart Waterfall Protocol, eliminating market sell-pressure.

"What protection exists if the parent entity fails?"

Rebuttal: All assets are held in ring-fenced Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs). Token holders are legally defined as Senior Secured Creditors with explicit redemption rights to hard assets.

"Is this another algorithmic stablecoin risk?"

Rebuttal: No. Stability is anchored in physical warehouse receipts (UGLD) and sovereign hardware (Varis OS), not speculative re-minting or debt-backed algorithmic code.

"Is the codebase secure and audited?"

Rebuttal: Protocol deployment is cryptographically locked until a Tier-1 independent firm and the Alpha Lead's audit team certify zero critical vulnerabilities via the ARP protocol.
