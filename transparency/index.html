Notice of Architectural Upgrades for Institutional Partners

Following Tier-1 institutional risk assessments, Powell Global LLC has instituted the following binding protocol amendments. 1. Institutional Custody & The LGE

To permanently eliminate founder liquidation risk and secure Day-1 market stability, the entirety of the 70,000,000,000 UNT supply has been minted and secured in audited offline cold storage. The protocol timeline officially shifts from a Token Generation Event (TGE) to a Liquidity Generation Event (LGE).

Prior to Month 3 market distribution, the total token supply—strictly excluding the 2.75% retained for internal development—will be transferred directly to the Alpha Lead Exchange Partner’s institutional custody. Powell Global LLC operates strictly as a passive facilitator; the Exchange Partner autonomously controls the release of the circulating supply via smart contract, scaling it against provided liquidity depth to mathematically enforce the $0.05 USD entry peg.

2. Cryptographic Vesting & Anti-Dump Execution

Powell Global LLC retains self-custody of a strict 2.75% of the supply, allocated specifically as the 1.75% Founder Share and a 1.00% Team/Hiring Pool. To facilitate strategic enterprise scaling, the 1.00% Team Pool is mathematically fragmented by the smart contract into 20 probationary hiring units of exactly 0.05% per new hire.

To cryptographically eliminate the possibility of a market dump, all retained allocations (both Founder and Team) are inextricably bound to the "Smart Waterfall" Protocol. This smart contract utilizes a "Yield-First" logic that prioritizes staking rewards over principal liquidation. The principal UNT allocations remain 100% locked; the contract only permits bi-monthly execution payouts via generated rewards. This ensures absolute zero sell pressure on the open market by the founding team.

3. Cryptographic Audit Lock

The execution of Tranche 1 capitalization acts as the immediate catalyst for the 60-Day Audit Remediation Protocol (ARP). The protocol deployer function remains cryptographically locked until the Alpha Lead's internal audit team and an independent Tier-1 firm certify zero Critical or Major vulnerabilities within the tokenomics, fee logic, and UGLD reserve contracts.

4. Algorithmic Fee-Scaling & Sovereign Floor

The smart contract logic has been updated to support API integrations with the Alpha Lead Exchange. While the standard baseline protocol fee initializes at 1.25%, merchant nodes integrated directly through the Alpha Lead's payment rails are algorithmically recognized by the smart contract. These specific nodes receive a matched 1.00% onboarding rate, programmed with an exclusive epoch-based glide path down to a 0.65% Sovereign Floor.

5. Tranche 1 Seniority & Liquidation Bar

To protect the liquidity pools managed by the Alpha Exchange Node, smart contracts enforce Tranche 1 Seniority Rights. The protocol cryptographically bars Powell Global LLC from executing corporate treasury liquidations during any recognized market decoupling event, prioritizing the $0.05 peg stability over corporate equity extraction.
