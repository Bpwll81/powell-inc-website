Corporate Structure: Unity Protocol is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Powell Global LLC. Powell Incorporated operates as a DBA of Powell Global LLC. All proprietary software and distributed ledger architectures remain the intellectual property of the parent entity.

Non-Security Classification: The Unity Protocol (UNT) is strictly classified as a Consumptive Utility Token. It is designed solely for network governance, node participation, and hardware infrastructure settlement. UNT does not represent an investment contract, an expectation of profit from the efforts of others, or an equity stake in any entity. It is a functional tool for the Varis OS ecosystem.

Asset Custody & Verification: Unity Gold (UGLD) serves as a digital warehouse receipt for physical commodities. All underlying precious metals are held in Bail-Free, third-party insured vaults. Powell Global LLC maintains full Auditory Compliance; physical reserves are subject to regular, independent inspections to verify 1:1 digital-to-physical parity.

Regulatory Compliance: This ecosystem is engineered for global institutional standards, operating in alignment with the EU MiCA Regulation (2023/1114), the Colorado Digital Token Act (SB 19-023), and ISO 20022 financial messaging protocols.
