Structural Note: The 5% Copper Floor

Copper represents the "industrial pulse" of our protocol. While Gold and Silver carry primary monetary status, Copper is essential for the electrification of the global grid. We maintain a 5% allocation to ensure Unity is tethered to physical infrastructure development.

Regulatory & Tax Complexity

Note: Unlike Gold (XAU), Copper is classified as an industrial base metal. Depending on jurisdiction, the acquisition and delivery of physical copper may trigger Value Added Tax (VAT) or Sales Tax. The Unity Treasury manages these tax liabilities at the point of minting to ensure UGLD token-holders maintain a net-zero tax exposure on their digital holdings until physical redemption.
