The Rationale: Why 5% Copper?

Copper is the metal of human progress. While not a "precious" metal in the traditional sense, its necessity in energy grids and electronics makes it an essential anchor for a trade-finance protocol. We maintain a 5% strategic floor to ensure the reserve is tethered to physical infrastructure development while allowing the 95% precious-metal core to focus on pure wealth preservation and scarcity.
