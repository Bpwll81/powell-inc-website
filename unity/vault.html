The 7-Year Promise

Retention & Wealth Building Vault

How It Works

The "7-Year Lock" is a smart contract on the XDC Network. It is designed to reward long-term loyalty with real asset accumulation.

  • Allocation: A percentage of monthly profits is converted to Gold (PAXG/Xaut).
  • The Lock: These assets are deposited into a transparent vault that cannot be withdrawn for 84 months (7 Years).
  • The Release: Upon maturity, the vault unlocks, providing the employee with a lump-sum asset that has hedged against inflation.

Why 7 Years?

7 years is a cycle. It is the difference between a job and a career. By locking these funds, we prevent short-term spending and force long-term wealth generation.
