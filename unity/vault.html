How the Vault Works

The "7-Year Lock" is an immutable smart contract. It is designed to reward your daily grind with a financial safety net that banks simply don't offer.

  • The Allocation: You choose a percentage of your tips/checks to auto-route into the vault. (10% is the protocol minimum).
  • The Gold Standard: Your funds are instantly converted into UGLD—fully backed by physical, inflation-resistant gold.
  • The Lock: The smart contract locks your deposit. You cannot touch it, and we cannot touch it, for exactly 84 months.
  • The Release: At Year 7, the vault unlocks, handing you a massive, inflation-hedged lump sum to buy a house, start a business, or retire early.
