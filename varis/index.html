The Varis Project

A multi-layered institutional blockchain architecture designed to eliminate friction across hospitality and commercial real estate operations.

OPERATING INFRASTRUCTURE POWERED BY THE UNITY PROTOCOL

The HR & Payroll Arm

Designed for real-time settlement. Employees define their own financial flow, with options to receive pay daily, weekly, or every other day. Varis automates the entire human resources lifecycle on-chain, eliminating legacy administrative bottlenecks.

Accounting & QuickBooks Arm

Blockchain precision meets legacy compliance. This arm integrates seamlessly into the Payroll tier and pushes data via direct API into QuickBooks. It provides real-time ledger balancing and tax alignment for high-volume commercial environments.

The XDC Network: Bridging Legacy Banking

The entire Varis Project is designed to eliminate the standard Net-10 and Net-30 holding patterns that cripple vendor liquidity. By utilizing the XDC Network, Varis achieves instant settlement finality on the blockchain while retaining full, frictionless interoperability with legacy fiat banking systems. Smart contracts ensure immediate vendor payout upon invoice approval.

Module H.C. (Hey Chef)

Our proprietary Food & Beverage Operating System. Built natively for the Mercantile's high-volume artisan environment, Hey Chef manages seamless point-of-sale, multi-vendor inventory management, and real-time kitchen logistics on the blockchain.
